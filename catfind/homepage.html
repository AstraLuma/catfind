catfind: sphinx indexer

This will help you find your python docs!

Try this: 

{{request.host_url}}py/package.Class.method

  • {{request.host_url}}py/collections.Counter.most_common
  • {{request.host_url}}py/importlib.metadata.entry_points
  • {{request.host_url}}py/webbrowser

Will also give you plain text or JSON if you ask for it.
